What this is

ETFfy stores end-of-day prices and ranks movers by daily, weekly, and month-to-date percentage changes. The focus is exchange-traded funds (ETFs) and similar listed funds. Where possible we keep the ticker (and ISIN) you would use in data providers, so rows stay comparable across lists.

Rankings are computed from saved snapshots (no live market calls on the report pages). External Link cells open Morningstar or the Financial Times when a stable URL exists, or show “Not available”.

Three coverage sets

  • Aviva Pension Manager

    Funds tied to your Aviva watchlist (SEDOL / list codes). Links prefer Financial Times fund summaries (GBP) when we have a suitable ISIN.

  • HSBC — Dubai online

    The HSBC listed-fund set available through the Dubai online channel (watchlist codes in the app). External links use Morningstar where we can resolve a symbol or ISIN.

  • Broad universe (~5,000 funds)

    A large directory of ETFs pulled from the Yahoo Finance screener (thousands of tickers), refreshed on a schedule. Good for market-wide movers; not tied to a single bank or pension list.
